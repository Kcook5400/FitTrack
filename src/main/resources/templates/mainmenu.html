Welcome to FitTrack!

FitTrack is a program to help you with your fitness routine!

Using FitTrack simply click on the navigation bar for whatever you want to log. It will take you to a menu where you can enter whatever it is you have chosen to log. From there you can view all of the data you have logged in FitTrack! FitTrack keeps track of your exercises, meals, and your weight and sleep progress!

Made an error? That's not a problem! FitTrack allows you to alter the information you entered! Never worry about typos again!
Want to delete a record? FitTrack allows you to do that too!
